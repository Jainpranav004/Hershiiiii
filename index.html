Dear Mom & Dad,

Happy Anniversary to the two people who made our house a home. Growing up, I didn’t realize how much effort it takes to keep a family happy and together — but now I understand that behind everything peaceful in our lives was always both of you. From small arguments to shared laughter, from stressful days to happy celebrations, you’ve handled life side by side in your own imperfect but beautiful way. Thank you for being my constant support system, my comfort place, and the reason our family feels complete. Also, thank you for proving that partnership mostly means patience… and sometimes pretending to agree just to avoid another argument 😄. I’m really lucky to call you my parents. Always proud of you both.

With all our love❤️,

--Gungun and Risha
